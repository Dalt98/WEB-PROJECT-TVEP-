About us

We aim to be the best possible resource for current and future college students of all types. TVEP puts you on the right path by linking you to information and services that helps anyone learn business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve personal and professional goals. Through individual, corporate, academic and government subscriptions, members have access to our facility of engaging, top-quality courses taught by recognized industry experts.

For 20 years, TVEP has helped students, leaders, IT and design pros, project managers—anyone in any role—build software, creative, and business skills. Now part of Linkedin, TVEP serves more than 10,000 organizations. TVEP is a global platform for success.
